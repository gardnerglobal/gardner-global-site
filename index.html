About
Gardner Global Communications

A Los Angeles-based international PR consultancy serving TV production companies, content distributors, streaming platforms, live entertainment producers and digital content creators.

Founded by Charlie Gardner, with more than 20 years of entertainment PR experience — including serving on the Group Executive Committee of Endemol Shine Group, then the world's largest independent production company with operations in more than 20 countries.

Gardner Global Communications provides senior communications counsel that builds visibility, protects reputation and delivers measurable results — across the U.S. and internationally.

Background
Charlie Gardner

Prior to founding Gardner Global Communications in Los Angeles in 2019, Charlie Gardner served as Global Communications Director at Endemol Shine Group — then the world's largest independent production company, with operations spanning more than 100 companies across 20+ countries. As a member of the Group Executive Committee, he helped set the strategic direction of the business at the highest level.

Charlie began his communications career at an international public affairs consultancy, advising corporations on political and legislative developments across the UK and globally. He went on to play a central role in the launch of UK broadcaster Channel 5, leading the network's corporate communications from the ground up.

Today, Charlie works directly with clients across the U.S., UK, Europe, the Middle East, and Asia — senior counsel at every stage, with no junior handoffs.
